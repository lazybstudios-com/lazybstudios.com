Golden Days

The digital baby book for busy moms. Record first steps, daily thoughts, and every tiny milestone as your baby grows — all in one private, secure timeline.

In Active Development

Overview

Golden Days was built for moms who want to capture the fleeting moments of early childhood without the pressure of social media or the clutter of physical scrapbooks. It's a calm, private space to record the thoughts that come to you during a midnight feeding, the first time they roll over, or just the funny face they made at breakfast.

Capture the story of your baby's growth, one day at a time.

Designed for Moms

  • Quick Thoughts — Jot down a memory in seconds, before you forget the feeling.
  • Photo Timeline — Your favorite baby photos organized by date and milestone.
  • Private & Secure — Your data is yours. No public feeds, no "likes," just your family history.
  • Circle of Caregivers — Selectively share specific memories with grandparents or your partner.

The Circle of Caregivers

Golden Days lets you decide exactly who sees what. Want to send a video of the first steps to Grandma, but keep your private postpartum reflections to yourself? The Circle of Caregivers allows you to build a trusted list of family and friends and choose which entries they can view. Sharing is per-item and revocable at any time.

Privacy & data

We take family privacy seriously. Your journal entries and photos are stored securely via Supabase with full encryption. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time through our data deletion portal.
