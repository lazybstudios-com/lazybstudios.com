Firewood Delivery

Streamlined logistics for seasonal firewood suppliers. Manage orders, optimize delivery routes, and track inventory in a single, lightweight tool.

In Active Development

Overview

Firewood Delivery is a specialized tool for local suppliers who need to manage the seasonal surge of customer orders. Instead of juggling texts and paper maps, suppliers can centralize their entire operation — from the initial customer request to the final drop-off.

The goal is simple: spend less time planning routes and more time delivering wood.

Key Features

  • Order Management — Capture customer details, wood type, and quantity in one searchable list.
  • Route Optimization — Lightweight GPS routing that groups deliveries to minimize fuel and time.
  • Inventory Tracking — Keep tabs on seasoned vs. green wood stock levels.
  • Customer History — Quickly pull up previous orders and delivery instructions for repeat customers.
  • Proof of Delivery — Attach photos of the drop-off location and capture customer signatures on-site.

Efficiency for Local Operators

Local suppliers often operate with thin margins. Firewood Delivery helps protect those margins by reducing deadhead miles and ensuring that every delivery is accounted for. It's built for use in the field, with a high-contrast interface and simplified data entry.

Privacy & data

Customer records and delivery data are stored securely via Supabase with full encryption. We respect your business data and do not share it with third parties or competitors. You can manage or delete your data at any time through our data deletion portal.
