Bill Reminder

Every recurring payment in one clean dashboard — with smart alerts that never let one slip past you.

Coming Soon to Google Play

Overview

Bill Reminder is a focused, minimal-friction app for the one job that keeps showing up in your inbox — remembering when bills are due. It takes the cognitive load of recurring payments off your shoulders and replaces it with a calm, organized dashboard plus alerts that arrive at exactly the right time.

No bank logins. No spreadsheets. No ad-supported nonsense. You add a bill once and Bill Reminder handles the rest.

What it does

  • Smart due-date alerts — nudges arrive a few days before each bill, sized to give you time to act without nagging.
  • Recurring bill management — monthly, quarterly, annual, or custom intervals, all handled automatically.
  • Quick-view dashboard — see what’s coming up this week, this month, and beyond in a single clean list.
  • Categorization & tagging — group bills by type (utilities, subscriptions, insurance) for at-a-glance budgeting.
  • Mark paid in one tap — close the loop without leaving the app or fiddling with checkmarks.
  • Privacy-first design — we never ask for bank credentials or payment card data. Bill Reminder tracks when bills are due, not how you pay them.

What it doesn’t do

Bill Reminder is deliberately not a budgeting app, a bank aggregator, or a money-management platform. We don’t want your account credentials. We don’t want to upsell you on credit products. The app does one thing and stays out of your way the rest of the time.

Privacy & data

Your bill data is stored securely via Supabase with encryption in transit and at rest, tied to your account so it’s available across your devices. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time through our data deletion portal. Full details in the Privacy Policy.
