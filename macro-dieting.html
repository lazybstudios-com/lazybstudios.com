Macro Dieting

Macro-tracking, simplified. Focus on your weekly planning and nutritional precision without the burnout of per-meal logging.

In Active Development

Overview

Macro Dieting is built for people who are serious about their nutrition but tired of apps that demand constant attention. Instead of forcing you to log every bite as you take it, Macro Dieting focuses on weekly planning and high-level precision.

Get the data you need to reach your fitness goals without the friction of traditional trackers.

Key Features

  • Weekly Macro Planning — Map out your week in advance to ensure you hit your targets.
  • Streamlined Logging — Quick-add meals and ingredients with a focus on speed.
  • Dynamic Adjustments — The app helps you adjust your remaining day or week based on what you've already eaten.
  • Progress Insights — Data-driven charts that show your consistency and trend over time.

The Weekly Strategy

Most diet apps fail because they are too reactive. Macro Dieting is proactive. By planning your macros on a weekly cycle, you allow for more flexibility. If you have a high-calorie dinner on Friday, the app helps you balance your intake across Saturday and Sunday to stay on track for your long-term goals.

Privacy & data

Your health data is personal. We store your targets and logs securely via Supabase with industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your health information to third parties. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time through our data deletion portal.
