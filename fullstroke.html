FullStroke

A complete repair shop in your pocket — work orders, customer records, parts, and invoices, with SMS customer updates that go out automatically.

In Active Development

Overview

FullStroke is purpose-built for the independent mechanic and small repair shop — outdoor power equipment, small engines, marine, powersports, and the long tail of speciality repair where the mainstream automotive shop-management software is overkill and the spreadsheets are a mess.

One app, one customer record, one running list of what needs to happen today.

What it handles

  • Work order management — create, track, and close repair jobs with status, photos, and notes attached.
  • Customer records — every customer, every machine, every job they’ve brought you, in a searchable history.
  • Parts inventory — track parts on hand, reorder thresholds, and which parts went into which job.
  • Invoicing & estimates — professional-looking invoices generated from the work order itself — no double entry.
  • Automated SMS updates — customers get a text when their machine is checked in, diagnosed, ready for pickup, or has a quote ready. Powered by the Twilio API.
  • Shop dashboard — what’s in the bay, what’s waiting on parts, what’s ready for pickup, all in one view.

Why automated SMS

Half the phone calls in a small repair shop are customers asking “is my mower ready yet?” FullStroke replaces those calls with a short, automatic text at each status change. Customer phone numbers are passed to the Twilio API solely for message delivery — we don’t store carrier data or use the number for any other purpose. Customers can reply STOP to opt out at any time.

Privacy & data

Shop data, customer records, and inventory are stored securely via Supabase. Shop owners control their own data and can export or delete it through our data deletion portal. Full details in the Privacy Policy.
