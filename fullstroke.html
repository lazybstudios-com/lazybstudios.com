Full Stroke

A complete repair shop in your pocket — work orders, customers, and parts, with an in-app AI assistant on tap.

In Active Development

Overview

Full Stroke is purpose-built for the independent mechanic and small repair shop — outdoor power equipment, small engines, marine, powersports, and the long tail of speciality repair where the mainstream automotive shop-management software is overkill and the spreadsheets are a mess.

One app, one customer record, one running list of what needs to happen today.

What it handles today

  • Work order tickets — create, track, and close repair jobs.
  • Customer records — searchable history of every customer and the work you’ve done for them.
  • Parts — track the parts you stock and the parts you use.
  • AI assistant — an in-app helper for shop owners, baked right into the workflow.
  • Shop dashboard — what’s in the bay, what’s waiting, what’s ready, all in one view.

On the roadmap

Modules planned but not yet live: full invoicing & estimates generated from work orders, reorder thresholds and supplier records for parts, and automated customer SMS updates (“your mower is ready for pickup”) once a third-party SMS provider is integrated. None of these are wired in yet — they’re on the build list.

Privacy & data

Shop data, customer records, and parts are stored securely via Supabase. Shop owners control their own data and can export or delete it through our data deletion portal. Full details in the Privacy Policy.

In active development

Full Stroke is being built with a small group of pilot shops. If you run a repair business and want to be part of the rollout, request a demo and we’ll set up a call.
