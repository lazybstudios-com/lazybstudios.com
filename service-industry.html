Service Industry App

The all-in-one operations tool for small service businesses. Job scheduling, customer records, and on-the-go invoicing for independent trades.

In Active Development

Overview

The Service Industry App is built for the "pro on the go" — lawn care providers, handymen, mobile detailers, and other independent tradespeople. It replaces the messy stack of clipboards, calendar apps, and separate invoicing tools with a single, integrated workflow.

Professionalize your business and save hours of administrative work every week.

Core Capabilities

  • Dynamic Scheduling — A clear calendar view of your upcoming jobs, with easy drag-and-drop rescheduling.
  • Customer CRM — Maintain detailed records for every client, including service history, preferences, and contact info.
  • Mobile Invoicing — Generate and send professional invoices immediately after the job is done.
  • Payment Tracking — See which invoices are paid, pending, or overdue at a glance.
  • Job Notes & Photos — Attach before-and-after photos and internal notes to every job record.

Built for the Field

We know that service pros aren't sitting at a desk. That's why the app is designed for one-handed use, with large buttons and a simplified interface that works even when you're wearing gloves or dealing with bright sunlight. Get the business data you need without the friction of a complex ERP.

Privacy & data

Your business and customer data are stored securely via Supabase with enterprise-grade encryption. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. You retain full ownership and can export or delete your records at any time through our data deletion portal.
