Golf Tracker

Your official World Handicap System index, your scorecards, and your foursome — in one app.

Coming Soon to Google Play

Overview

Golf Tracker calculates and maintains your official World Handicap System index so you can post rounds, watch your game improve, and play competitive matches with friends across any course in the country.

Built by golfers who got tired of paper scorecards and clunky club apps, Golf Tracker keeps the post-round bookkeeping fast and the social side fun.

What it does

  • Official WHS handicap index — calculated using current World Handicap System formulas, updated after every round.
  • Course database with slope & rating — tees, slope, and course rating pre-loaded so your differentials are always correct.
  • Score posting in seconds — hole-by-hole or total-score entry, with adjusted gross score calculated for you.
  • Social Score Tracking — share scores and handicaps within user-created groups (weekend foursome, league, club, family) without making anything public.
  • Group rounds & leaderboards — play matches with friends, see live standings, and settle the bets honestly.
  • Round history & trends — spot the holes that quietly cost you strokes over time.

Social, not public

Social Score Tracking is opt-in and group-scoped. You see the scores of people in groups you joined or created — nothing more. We don’t publish leaderboards, sell scores to handicap services, or share data with advertisers. You can leave a group at any time, and you can revoke a single round’s visibility without affecting your overall index.

Privacy & data

Scores, handicap data, and group memberships are stored securely via Supabase. You can export your full round history or permanently delete it — or just your golf history while keeping the rest of your account — via our data deletion portal. Full details in the Privacy Policy.
