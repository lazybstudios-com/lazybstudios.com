Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 14, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how Lazy B Studios LLC (“Lazy B Studios,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles refund requests across our product lineup. Different categories of products carry different refund terms, summarized below.

1. Public Apps (Bill Reminder, Golf Tracker)

Our public consumer apps are distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Any paid features, in-app purchases, or subscriptions tied to these apps are billed and processed by Google or Apple respectively.

  • Google Play purchases: Refund requests are governed by Google’s refund policy and must be submitted through Google Play (support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637).
  • Apple App Store purchases: Refund requests are handled by Apple via reportaproblem.apple.com.

We will assist where we reasonably can, but Lazy B Studios LLC cannot directly refund a purchase processed by a third-party app store.

2. Enterprise & In-Development Solutions

For our enterprise products — Chemflo Suite, Full Stroke, Golden Days, and Signops Pro — refund terms are defined in the individual service agreement, master services agreement, or order form signed with the client.

Unless otherwise stated in your agreement, paid enterprise engagements are eligible for a 14-day money-back guarantee from the date of initial subscription activation, prorated against any usage. Implementation, custom development, and onboarding fees are generally non-refundable once work has begun.

3. Research & Development Prototypes

Projects in our Research & Development category — SampleFlow, Firewood Delivery, Macro Dieting, and the Service Industry App — are not currently sold to the public. No payment is collected, and therefore no refund applies. If a prototype graduates into a paid product, terms will be updated here before billing begins.

4. How to Request a Refund

For purchases that fall within our direct control (enterprise subscriptions, implementation fees, etc.), email support@lazybstudios.com with:

  • Your full name and the email associated with the account
  • The product or invoice number
  • The reason for the request

We will acknowledge receipt within 5 business days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days of approval.
